# Plan 294 — GOAT Gate G2: ICT Inflection Location (Synthetic NPC Suite)

**Date:** 2026-06-19
**Status:** ⚠️ BORDERLINE-FAIL — median 37.5%, outside [5%, 20%]. Documented; not blocking.

## Verdict

**G2 BORDERLINE-FAIL.** The paper's ~10% inflection (arxiv 2606.19771 §A.4.1,
measured on LLM next-token distributions) does **not** transfer to the
synthetic-NPC decision suite. The median inflection sits at 37.5%, with IQR
25% – 50%. This is the failure mode Plan §Risks anticipates; see
[Issue 033](../.issues/033_ict_g2_inflection_37_percent_npc_domain.md) for
the full analysis.

G2 does **not** block G3 (the make-or-break). Per Plan §Implementation Order
the Super-GOAT-vs-Gain decision point is after G3, not G2. G2 borderline-fail
just means callers sweep `k_percent` empirically rather than hard-coding 0.10.

## Interpretation

The distribution is **multi-modal**:

- A peak at **10%** (243 / 1000): the LLM-style sharp inflection. These are
  the "committed" regime decision points where 1 trajectory diverges sharply
  from the other 7.
- A peak at **50%** (316 / 1000): no clean inflection. These are "undecided"
  and "noise" regime decision points where the uniqueness curve is flatter.
- Spread between 30%-60%: the second-difference maximum lands mid-range when
  there's no sharp elbow.

This is consistent with the regime mixture: committed decisions show the
paper's 1-vs-K structure (10% inflection); undecided/noise decisions don't.

## Why this isn't a hard failure

1. **Paper caveat:** §A.4.1 measures on LLM vocabularies (32K+ tokens with
   sharp long-tail structure). NPC action distributions are small (6-32
   discrete actions) with less bimodal structure.
2. **Plan §Risks anticipated this:** "The 10% is LLM-token-specific. Sweep
   k% to find our inflection. May be 20-30% for NPCs."
3. **k_percent is a runtime parameter:** `BranchingDetector::new` takes
   `k_percent` explicitly. Default stays at 0.10 (paper value); NPC callers
   pass 0.38 (or sweep for their workload).
4. **G3 is the actual arbiter:** Plan §Implementation Order places the
   Super-GOAT decision point after G3, not G2.

## Recommendation for callers

- **LLM workloads** (Bebop H_1→H_2 upgrade, Curiosity Pulse on token
  distributions): keep `k_percent = 0.10` (paper default).
- **NPC workloads** (riir-ai Plan 324 runtime fusion): start with
  `k_percent = 0.38` (this Issue's measurement), sweep on real game data.

## What this does NOT prove

- That the ICT primitives are wrong. They compute the right math; the
  inflection is a property of the input distribution shape, not the
  implementation.
- That G3 will fail. G3 measures a different property (orthogonality to H_1).
